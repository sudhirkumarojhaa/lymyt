About us

Located in Coney Island Ave, Lymyt Gym is the premiere place for new circuit and interval training styles with the right equipment to match. With our top of the line equipment, clean facility, and a friendly-knowledgeable staff – You are one step closer to your fitness goals. Stop by today and let us help you get there. Pick a plan that's right for you and our knowledgeable staff will help you meet your goals based around your schedule. No matter where you are in your fitness goals, signing up to your local gym is a great first step into elevating that goal. Our mission here at Lymyt Gym is to make sure you meet that goal. Whether you're interested in Crossfit style training, powerlifting, body building, cardio workouts, or simply just looking to get into shape – We've got you covered. With the right equipment and a little motivation, you will go far. Come see us today, the door is always open.!
